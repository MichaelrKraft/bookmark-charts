How it works — 2 minutes to your story.

No account. No upload. The whole thing runs in your browser.

Export your bookmarks

In Chrome: chrome://bookmarks → ⋮ → Export. You get a bookmarks.html file. (Works with Firefox, Safari, Arc, Brave, Edge too.)

Drop it in

On the next page, drag your file onto the drop zone — or click to pick it. We parse it in your browser in about 3 seconds. Nothing leaves your machine.

See who you've been

Archetype assigned. Stats tallied. Graph built. Share card ready. Explore your tabs, timelines, and the graveyard of good intentions.

🔒
Actually private. Not "we promise" private.

The entire app is static HTML + JavaScript. When you drop your file, it's parsed locally by your browser. No network requests, no telemetry, no account. Open DevTools and watch — the Network tab is empty. Open-source soon.
